    Tips

    Achieving a lifestyle of zero waste and minimalism can be very rewarding. Here are a couple of tips to help you get started. Declutter, Develop, and Designate.

    According to the Marie Kondo method, go through each item and see if "it sparks joy." If it does, it is worth keeping, but if it doesn't it should be given to someone who it will spark joy for. This skills will continue to develop experiment with different decluttering strategies. Finally, you will be able to design a system in which is fully functional to help you declutter your different possessions. It is important to note that consistency is key when it comes to decluttering, in that it is an ongoing process!
